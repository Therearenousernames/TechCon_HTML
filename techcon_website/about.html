Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts eager to share ideas and innovations. Over the years, it has grown into a global event, attracting industry leaders, startups, and researchers. Each year, the conference continues to expand its reach and impact on the tech world.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and drive technological progress that shapes the future. We believe in empowering individuals and organizations to push boundaries and create solutions for tomorrow’s challenges.

Notable Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted some of the most influential figures in technology, who have shared their insights and inspired audiences worldwide.

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Renowned as the world’s first computer programmer, Dr. Lovelace’s pioneering work laid the foundation for modern computing. At TechCon, she inspired audiences with her vision of a future powered by algorithms.

Alan Turing

Known as the father of computer science, Alan Turing’s contributions to cryptography and artificial intelligence have shaped modern technology. His keynote highlighted the ethical responsibilities of innovation.

Grace Hopper

A trailblazer in computer programming, Grace Hopper advocated for accessible and user-friendly software. Her talk emphasized the importance of inclusivity and breaking barriers in technology.
